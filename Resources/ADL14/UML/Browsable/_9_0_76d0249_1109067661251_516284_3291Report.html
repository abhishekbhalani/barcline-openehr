Package rm::data_types::quantity::date_time

The data_types.quantity.date_time package includes three absolute date/time concepts: DV_DATE, DV_TIME, DV_DATE_TIME, a relative concept: DV_DURATION, and the concept of partial dates and times, via DV_PARTIAL_DATE, DV_PARTIAL_TIME.
